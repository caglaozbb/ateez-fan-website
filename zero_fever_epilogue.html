ZERO : FEVER PART EPILOGUE

  
        Zero : Fever Epilogue is the eighth mini album by ATEEZ. It was released on December 10, 2021 with "Turbulence" and "The Real" serving as the album's double title tracks.
        The MV for "Turbulence" was pre-released on December 3, 2021.
        
        The physical album comes in three versions: A, Diary, and Z.
