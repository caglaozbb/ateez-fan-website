ZERO : FEVER PART 2

  
  Zero: Fever Part.2 is the sixth extended play (EP) by South Korean boy group Ateez. 
  It was released on March 1, 2021, with the track "Fireworks (I'm the One)" serving as lead single. 
  The physical album comes in three versions: A, Diary, and Z, and received over 350,000 pre-orders, exceeding their previous release, Zero: Fever Part.1, by over 100,000 orders.
  The album is composed of seven tracks, and incorporates different genres such as hip hop, EDM, and synth-pop.

  Commercially, the album debuted at number one on South Korea's Gaon Album Chart, 
  and number eight on Billboard's World Album Chart. The album's lead single, "Fireworks (I'm the One)", 
  is Ateez's first song to chart on the Gaon Digital Chart, peaking at 148.
